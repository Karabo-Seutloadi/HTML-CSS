We Empower the Youth of South Africa

SAYIPA is a dynamic organization dedicated to transforming the property sector in South Africa by accelerating the active participation of young people in the real estate industry.

At SAYIPA, we recognize the immense potential of the youth and understand the challenges young individuals face when attempting to participate in the property sector.

SAYIPA was founded with a mission to empower and inspire young individuals to become key contributors in shaping the property sector through comprehensive training programs designed to equip the youth with the knowledge, skills, and tools necessary to excel in this ever-evolving industry.
